**SecurePass** is a state-of-the-art password management and generation suite designed with a futuristic "Batman-Noir" aesthetic and cryptographic precision. It's not just a tool; it's a visual experience powered by a custom **Electrified Shield Lattice Engine**.

---

Live link :- **https://zsecpass.netlify.app/**
## ⚡ Key Modules

### 🔍 Real-time Entropy Analyzer
*   **Instant Feedback**: Watch the shield strength fluctuate with dynamic color shifts as you type.
*   **Advanced Metrics**: Evaluates length, character variety, and entropy density.
*   **Crack Time Estimation**: Get realistic estimates of brute-force resistance.

### 🎲 High-Entropy Generator
*   **CSPRNG Powered**: Uses `window.crypto.getRandomValues()` for true cryptographic randomness.
*   **Customizable Complexity**: Adjust length (8–64 chars) and toggle Uppercase, Lowercase, Numbers, and Symbols.
*   **Ambiguity Filter**: Option to exclude confusing characters (like `i, l, 1, L, o, 0, O`).

### 🌌 Electrified Shield Lattice (Visual Core)
*   **Interactive 3D Background**: A high-performance Three.js icosahedron lattice that responds to your input.
*   **Custom Shaders**: GLSL shaders creating cyan, purple, and green gradients with "electrical flicker" effects.

### 🔒 Permanent Local Vault
*   **Local Storage**: Save your passwords securely on your device with custom labels.
*   **Session History**: Keep track of recently generated strings during your active session.
*   **Export Support**: Easily export your history for backup.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Graphics Engine**: [Three.js](https://threejs.org/) (WebGL 2.0)
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS3 with Glassmorphism & Custom Properties
- **Logic**: ES6+ JavaScript (Asynchronous & Modular)
- **Typography**: [Outfit](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit) & [JetBrains Mono](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/JetBrains+Mono)
- **Icons**: Custom SVG Path Animations
